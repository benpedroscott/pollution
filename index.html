Trouble over dirtied water and air

in the Louisiana delta

by Ben Scott

The impact of the oil industry looms over Louisiana. The southern region of the state, known as Acadiana, has been victim to harmful petrochemical companies that stake their claim to the vast amounts of oil underneath the region’s land and marshes.

The impact hasn’t been done unto just the nature, but also unto the people of the region. Life expectancy has dropped and the cancer rate has skyrocketed in the region, hence a nickname for one particular stretch in Southeastern Louisiana: Cancer Alley.
