Industrial companies
that are polluting
Louisiana


Over 300 industrial facilities operate in Louisiana, accordin to the state's economic development agency. With a lack of strict regulation from the LDEQ, these companies far exceed the safe levels for the chemicals they emit. This sampling of four companies and their Louisiana-based facilities is just a taste of the 300-some number of facilities across the state.

Exxon Mobil

One of the largest oil refineries in the U.S. and in the World.

Denka

A neoprene plant and a major chloroprene emitter.

Formosa Plastics

A plastics complex that is planned to double a parish's toxic emissions.

Union Carbide

A major emitter of ethylene oxide, a carcinogenic gas.
